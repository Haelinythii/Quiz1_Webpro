Pasar Terapung

Pasar Terapung Muara Kuin or Pasar Terapung Sungai Barito is a traditional floating market located on the Barito river at the mouth of the Kuin river, Banjarmasin, South Kalimantan. Muara Kuin Floating Market is a saujana heirloom of Banjarmasin City. Traders and buyers use jukung, which is the name for boats in Banjarese. This market starts after Fajr prayer until after seven in the morning. The rising sun reflects the light between the transactions of vegetables and garden produce from the villages along the Barito river and its tributaries.

Bukit Teletubbies

Banjarmasin also has a panoramic view of the green New Zealand-style hills all year round, the name is Bukit Rimpi Pelaihari. Bukit Rimpi is a hill that is overgrown by green savanna which is decorated with a stretch of grass with a very beautiful view. This beautiful hill is better known as Teletubies Hill because of its towering mounds like in the Teletubies film.

Menara Pandang Banjarmasin

Menara Pandang is located on the bank of the Martapura River and close to a floating market in Siring to be precise on Jalan Kapten Pierre Tendean, Banjarmasin. This viewing tower has four floors. At the top or third floor of this tower is an open area. So, visitors can go up and enjoy the beautiful view of Banjarmasin City. This viewing tower is close to my house, only about 500 meters away.

Taman Wisata Pulau Kembang

Pulau Kembang is a land or delta in the middle of the Barito river. It is called an island, because of its fairly large area and on it there is a tourist forest covering an area of more than 50 hectares which can be used as a tourist destination. We can find many monkeys and various types of birds. One of the monkeys that we can meet on this island is proboscis monkey. Besides being able to get acquainted with friendly monkeys, we can also take pictures in the mangrove forest
